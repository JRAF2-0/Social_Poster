Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Luna Team Social Poster, operated by LUNA AI SYSTEM OPC ("we", "our", or "us"), a company based in the Philippines. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our social media automation service.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: TikTok, Facebook, and Instagram account credentials and access tokens obtained through official OAuth authorization flows.
  • Content Data: Videos, images, captions, and scheduling information that you provide for posting.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our service, including post history and status logs.
  • Contact Information: Email address if you contact us directly.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our social media automation service.
  • Post content to your authorized social media accounts on your behalf.
  • Track and report the status of your scheduled posts.
  • Respond to your comments and questions.
  • Improve and optimize our service.

4. TikTok API Data

Our application uses the TikTok Content Posting API. By authorizing our app, you grant us permission to post content to your TikTok account on your behalf. We only access the permissions you explicitly grant us. We do not sell, share, or distribute your TikTok data to any third parties.

We collect and use the following TikTok data:

  • Access tokens for posting content on your behalf.
  • Creator information (username, nickname) for verification purposes.
  • Post status and publish IDs for tracking purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

We store your access tokens and content data securely. We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Access tokens are stored securely with restricted access and are never shared with unauthorized parties.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services. Access tokens are refreshed regularly and expired tokens are deleted. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with the following third-party platforms:

  • TikTok — for video posting via TikTok Content Posting API
  • Facebook/Instagram — for posting via Facebook Graph API
  • Cloudinary — for media storage
  • Google Sheets — for content calendar and configuration

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Revoke authorization of our app from your social media accounts at any time.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.
